Ensembl

Cookware that goes beyond the kitchen

Ensembl is taking a design-forward approach to re-imagining the tools we use for modern living, beginning with a collection of radically redesigned cookware. When we were engaged to design and develop their new website, we embraced the chance to bring their resonant brand identity to life.

13

Website

Shop it like it’s hot

We worked to channel their progressive approach to design and social responsibility into the site, combining ecommerce, a lifestyle blog, and a way to participate in their sustainability program. To connect with Ensembl’s core audience, we embraced their editorial point of view, combining relevant, helpful content while giving the products a stylized treatment that echoed the brand’s identity. We paired modern typography with oversized imagery, showcasing the minimal design and innovative features while emphasizing their inherent beauty.

23

Ecommerce

Built to perform

We took an innovative approach to the ecommerce solution, pairing two of our favorite platforms and letting each play to its strengths. The website is built on Shopify and leverages its excellent operational capabilities, while the content management system is built within Wordpress, allowing us to deliver a robust set of tools that empower Ensembl to manage the website with ease.

33

Blog

Cooking up content

For Ensembl, cookware is really just the beginning. With an exciting range of innovative homeware products on the horizon, they've positioned themselves as a lifestyle brand that's ready to grow with their collection. The website takes an editorial approach to content, with the blog being their most valuable opportunity to engage with new audiences. We developed a robust toolkit to support their creative team, providing the flexibility to craft truly unique stories designed to capture attention and bring users back for more.
