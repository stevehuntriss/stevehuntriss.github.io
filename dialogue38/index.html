Branding

When your designs are your brand

Dialogue 38’s award-winning portfolio is defined by a luxe style that perfectly meshes with our own. They’ve cultivated a legacy of intentional minimalism and crafted excellence, and we aimed to honor that through the branding as we developed a similarly polished yet minimal approach to their visual identity.

The right type

With an architectural design approach rooted in clean lines and modern influences, we looked to a type family that could compliment dialogue38’s distinct aesthetic. We embraced Neutraface and its open and unobtrusive feel that manages to fuse linear and geometric shapes with an unmistakably human element.

Website

Elevating the body of work

Inspired by the firm’s architectural styling, the website has taken a minimalist approach to both its content and UI, focusing on the portfolio showcase while allowing their impressive catalogue of awards to play a supporting role. Big, bold imagery tells a clear story, while dynamic interactions and unique animations add depth and dimension to experience.

Details make the difference

Project pages were the focal point of the website, intentionally designed to bring the details of dialogue38’s work to life, and with dramatic effect. A modular case-study builder empowers the creative team to craft portfolio entries that represent the ethos of each project they showcase.

Beyond responsive

These days, a mobile-friendly website is table stakes, and we’re not satisfied with just creating a standard mobile experience. We approached the design of each device deliberately, tailoring the experience for mobile, tablet, laptop, and desktop devices. No matter how dialogue38's audience engages with their portfolio, it’s designed to shine.
